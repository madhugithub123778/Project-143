Instructions

  1. First, move 3-4 feet away from your screen.
  2. Show your right wrist in front of the camera. A red dot should appear on it.
  3. After the dot appears, click the Start Playing button, to begin the game.
  4. Move your right wrist up and down to control the paddle.
  5. Points will be added to your bank when you hit the ball using the paddle.
